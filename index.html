About Tekathon 4.0

Tekathon 4.0 is Chandigarh University's premier tech innovation hackathon, an electrifying event where brilliant minds converge to push the boundaries of technology. It brings together diverse talents in AI, software development, and hardware engineering, challenging participants to collaborate and build impactful, real-world solutions that address contemporary global issues.

About CU

Chandigarh University is a leading hub of innovation and research, fostering creativity and entrepreneurship among thousands of students.

Vision

To cultivate an innovative ecosystem where students are empowered to develop impactful solutions to real-world challenges, harnessing the power of cutting-edge technology.

Mission

To seamlessly bridge the gap between industry demands and academic prowess through engaging hackathons, expert mentorship, and collaborative initiatives, preparing students for future success.
